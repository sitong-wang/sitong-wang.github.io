Sitong Wang

I am a final-year PhD student at Columbia CS , advised by Lydia Chilton. My work focuses on human-AI interaction, with an emphasis on multimodal and agentic AI. I design, build, and evaluate AI systems that support human creativity and productivity.

Previously, I interned twice at Adobe Research with Dingzeyu Li, Anh Truong, and Mira Dontcheva on the video research team. I've also collaborated with Xiaojuan Ma at HKUST CSE and Panagiotis Apostolellis at UVA CS.

I will be joining Microsoft Research Asia this fall as a Senior Researcher on the Future Media Experience team. If you are interested in collaborating or would like to connect, please feel free to reach out!
