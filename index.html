Sitong Wang

I am a final-year CS PhD student at Columbia University, advised by Prof. Lydia Chilton in the Computational Design Lab and the Data Agents Process Lab.

My research focuses on human-AI interaction. I design, build and evaluate AI systems that enhance human creativity, productivity, and learning.
