Sitong Wang

I am a Computer Science PhD candidate at Columbia University. I work with Prof. Lydia Chilton in the Computational Design Lab.

My research focuses on Human-AI interaction. I develop AI-powered systems to enhance human creativity and productivity.

I am on the job market for industry research positions. If you know of any suitable openings, please feel free to reach out!
